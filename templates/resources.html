Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is CropCare AI?

CropCare AI uses advanced machine learning models trained on thousands of images. Accuracy varies by disease but typically exceeds 85% when provided with clear images and accurate descriptions.

What image quality is needed?

Use clear, well-lit photos focused on the affected area. Avoid blurry or dark images. Include the entire leaf if possible, with good contrast between healthy and diseased tissue.

Is weather data required?

Weather data enhances prediction accuracy but is optional. If unavailable, you can use typical values for your region during the current season.

Can I use this for commercial farming?

Yes, CropCare AI is suitable for both small-scale and commercial farming. However, always consult with agricultural experts for critical decisions.

How often should I check my crops?

Regular weekly inspections are recommended, especially during the growing season. Increase frequency during periods of high disease risk (humid weather, after rain).
