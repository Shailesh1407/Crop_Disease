About CropCare AI

Empowering farmers with AI-driven crop disease detection and management solutions

Our Mission

CropCare AI is dedicated to helping farmers and agronomists detect crop diseases early, prevent losses, and improve yields through cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology. We combine image analysis, natural language processing, and weather data to provide accurate, actionable insights.

How It Works

Image Analysis

ResNet50 deep learning model extracts features from leaf images to identify visual disease patterns.

Text Understanding

BERT-based sentence transformers analyze symptom descriptions for context-aware predictions.

Weather Integration

Temperature, humidity, and rainfall data enhance prediction accuracy by considering environmental factors.

Multi-Modal AI

Combines all inputs through machine learning classifiers for robust disease identification.

Supported Crops

Currently, CropCare AI supports disease detection for:

  • Apple: Apple Scab, Black Rot, Cedar Rust, and Healthy detection
  • Corn: Common Rust, Northern Leaf Blight, Cercospora Leaf Spot, and Healthy detection

We are continuously expanding our database to include more crops and diseases.
