Get results in four simple steps

1. Upload Leaf Image

Use a clear, well-lit photo focused on the symptomatic region.

2. Add Description

Briefly describe what you see: color, spots, edges, curling.

3. Enter Weather

Provide temperature, humidity, and rainfall to improve accuracy.

4. Review Advisory

See the predicted disease, risk, and recommended next actions.
